How It Works

TF-IDF + Cosine Similarity

Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency weights terms by their importance in the corpus. Your query is vectorised and ranked by cosine similarity against all job documents.

BM25 (Okapi BM25)

A probabilistic ranking function that considers term frequency, document length normalisation, and an inverse document frequency weight — the standard for modern search engines.
